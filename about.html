About

A small studio, focused on the basics done well.

BrightPath Web Studio is based in Orem, Utah, and works with local service businesses, freelancers, and small startups across the country. We keep our projects tight so we can move fast and stay affordable.

Our Approach

Less framework, more clarity.

Most small businesses do not need a heavy page builder, a content management system with twenty plugins, or a six-week design phase. They need a clean, honest website that loads fast and explains what they do.

That is what we build. We start with a simple structure, write friendly copy, and ship a site you can launch the same week.

What we believe

Three simple ideas guide every project.

Clarity over cleverness

If a visitor cannot tell what you do in five seconds, the design failed. We write copy that says the obvious thing first.

Speed matters

Fast websites get more visitors, more calls, and more business. We build with plain HTML and CSS so nothing slows you down.

Honest scope

We say no to features you do not need. A starter site is a starter site. If you outgrow it, we will help you plan the next step.
